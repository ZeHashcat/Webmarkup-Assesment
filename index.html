Tokyo

Being The most populous prefecture of Japan, Tokyo is a very busy area. Tokyo used to be called Edo and in 1603 became the center of ruling for the famous shogun Tokugawa Ieyasu, because of this, Edo became one of the largest cities in the world at the time. Nowadays Tokyo consists of 62 municipalities, 23 special wards, 26 cities, 5 towns and 8 villages. Some famous places include Tokyo Station, Yurakucho, Shibuya, Harajuku and Akibahara.

Moscow

Standing on the Moskva River in Central Russia, Moscow has been inhabited since time immemorial. Moscow is home to the Kremlin, a fortified complex that has been the seat of grand dukes and tsars. It was also home to both Vladimir Lenin and Joseph Stalin, the latter of which replaced the golden eagles on the towers with Kremlin starts. With a population of 12.4 million residents Moscow is one of the largest cities in the world.
